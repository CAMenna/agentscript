Storm Outage — Inbound Voice
OUTAGE ACTIVE·Exelon Service Territory·~28,400 Customers Impacted·ETR: 11:30 PM CT
Script ID
OUT-STORM-01
1
Opening Greeting & Authentication
Required▼
"Thank you for calling Exelon — this is [your name] with Exelon Customer Care. I understand this may be a stressful time. May I please get your name and the service address or account number for your location?"
Auth note: Verify name + address OR name + last 4 of SSN. Do not require both during declared storm events — policy waiver is active.
2
Empathy Statement & Situation Acknowledgment
Empathy▼
"Mr. Agassi, I'm truly sorry you're without power, especially given the severe weather we've had today. I want to assure you that our crews are working around the clock — your safety is our top priority."
Use the customer's name. Avoid saying "I understand how you feel" — instead mirror the specific hardship (cold weather, medical equipment, food spoilage, etc.) if the customer mentions it.
3
Outage Confirmation & ETR Delivery
Required▼
"I can confirm there is an active outage affecting your area — this is part of the widespread storm damage impacting thousands of customers across our service territory. Our current estimated restoration time for your area is by 11:30 PM tonight, though crews are working hard and we hope to restore power sooner."
⚠️
Do not guarantee a specific time. Always use "by" or "estimated." If the customer presses for an exact time, say: "I want to be transparent — restoration depends on the extent of damage our crews find on-site, and I don't want to give you a time I can't stand behind."
Outage details on screen: Cause — High winds / downed lines. Affected: 28,400 customers. Crew dispatch status: Active. Crew count: 240+ field technicians.
4
Safety Screening
Required▼
"Before we continue, I want to ask a couple of important safety questions. Are there any downed power lines or sparking wires near your home or property? And do you or anyone in your household depend on electrically powered medical equipment?"
Branch Logic
Downed line →Instruct customer to stay back at least 30 feet. Do NOT approach. Call 911 immediately. Escalate to Priority Outage queue. Log safety concern.
Medical need →Flag account as Life Support. Offer priority ETR update via callback. Transfer to Medical Baseline team if warranted.
No concerns →Continue to Step 5.

"I've confirmed your outage is already on file — you don't need to take any further action to report it. I'd like to make sure you have a couple of easy ways to track progress: you can text OUT to 26633 for automated updates, or visit exeloncorp.com/outages for our live outage map."

  • Offer text notification enrollment if not already enrolled
  • Offer outage map link via text/email if customer requests
